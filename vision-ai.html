What is Vision AI?

Vision AI, or Computer Vision, is a technology that helps computers interpret and analyze visual data like images and videos. It mimics the way humans see, but it does so with precision and scale that humans can't match. From identifying objects to detecting patterns, Vision AI opens up endless possibilities for innovation.

Why Does Vision AI Matter?

We live in a world filled with visual information. Every second, billions of images and videos are captured globally. With Vision AI, businesses can unlock hidden insights from this visual data. Imagine automating tasks like identifying defective products on a production line or ensuring security through facial recognition. Vision AI makes these processes faster and smarter.
